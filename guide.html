For new members

1. Start your retirement well in advance

The first penny you save up can be worth seven kroner when you retire, while the last penny you save up may only be worth a penny. As many as 97 percent of the members of AkademikerPension will need the money in their pension savings. That is more than in the general population. Among other Danish women, 80 per cent and other Danish men 74 per cent have time to experience retirement age.

2. Get an overview of your pension

Your pension plan is usually linked to your job and an important part of your total salary. Therefore, when you get a new job, you typically also automatically get a pension scheme. It is paid through your salary and constitutes a fixed percentage, usually between 15 and 18 percent each month. Your employer pays two-thirds, and you pay the last third yourself.

3. Adjust your pension on an ongoing basis

We know that retirement is not the first thing that comes to mind when starting a new job. That is why we have made it easy for you to adjust your pension to suit you.
