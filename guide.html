For new members

1. Start your retirement well in advance

The first penny you save up can be worth seven kroner when you retire, while the last penny you save up may only be worth a penny. As many as 97 percent of the members of AkademikerPension will need the money in their pension savings. That is more than in the general population. Among other Danish women, 80 per cent and other Danish men 74 per cent have time to experience retirement age. At the same time, life expectancy is rising and you should expect to live on your pension money for more than 20 years.
